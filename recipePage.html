Christmas cookies

Laura Lees

23 December, 2023

Share:
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn
  • Pinterest

60 Minutes

Super Easy

35 cookies

Dessert

Star Rating

These vanilla cookies are very simple to make. Cut them into Christmas shapes and decorate them with icing and sugar sprinkles.

Ingredients:

125 g of butter, softened
50 g g of icing sugar
1 medium egg medium eggs
1 teaspoon teaspoons of vanilla extract
225 g g of plain flour

For the icing:

225 g g of icing sugar
2 tbsp of warm water

1 serving makes 35 cookies

You will also need:

You could add different flavours to your cookies. Here are some suggestions. Leave out the vanilla and, at step 4, add:

  • 1 teaspoon of ground cardamom or cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of ground ginger or orange zest
NOTE: These cookies will keep for up to 5 days in an airtight container.

Nutritional Information: You can replace the butter with dairy-free spread.

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    First, grease two baking trays. Then, put the butter in a large bowl. Beat it until it is smooth. Use a sieve to sift in the icing sugar. Beat again.

  2. Step 2

    Break the egg into a small bowl. Add the vanilla extract and beat well with a fork.

  3. Step 3

    Add the eggy mixture to the large bowl, a little at a time. Beat well between each addition.

  4. Step 4

    Add the flour and stir the mixture until it starts to form a dough. Then, use your hands to mix the dough and squeeze it into a ball.

  5. Step 5

    Wrap the dough in plastic foodwrap and put it in the fridge for 30 minutes. Heat the oven to 180°C , 350°F or Gas Mark 4.

  6. Step 6

    Unwrap the dough and put it onto a clean surface. Dust the surface and a rolling pin with flour. Roll it out until it is just thinner than your little finger. Use the cutters to cut out lots of shapes.

  7. Step 7

    Put the cookies onto the trays. Squeeze the scraps together and roll them out again. Cut out more shapes and put them on the trays.

  8. Step 8

    Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden. Leave on the trays for 5 minutes, then put on a wire rack to cool.

  9. Step 9

    For the icing, soft the icing sugar into a bowl and mix it with the warm water. Spread onto each cookie with a blunt knife. Scatter sugar sprinkles on top.
